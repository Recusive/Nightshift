# Daemon index records brain role, not which sub-agents were delegated

## Problem

The session index records every v2 session as role=brain. It doesn't capture which sub-agents were actually delegated (build, evolve, security, etc). This makes the session history useless for understanding what work was done.

Related to #210 (sessions-since counters broken).

## Fix

After the brain exits, the daemon should parse the raw log or decisions log to extract delegated sub-agent types. Record them in the index row, e.g.:

| date | session_id | role | delegations | exit | duration | cost |
|------|-----------|------|-------------|------|----------|------|
| 2026-04-08 | 20260408-183951 | brain | build,evolve | 0 | 20m | $1.37 |

## Acceptance Criteria

- Index shows which sub-agents were delegated per session
- Dashboard reads delegation history for accurate sessions-since counts
- Handoff template includes delegation summary
