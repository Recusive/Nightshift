## Context

The `tr -d '|\n\r'` strip applied to FEATURE and PR_URL in `daemon.sh` does not
remove tab characters. Tabs in FEATURE are handled harmlessly by `pick-role.py`'s
`.strip()` call on each cell, so this is not a bug. However the omission is
non-obvious and could confuse a future developer who wonders why tabs were left out.

Flagged in PR #156 review as "not blocking, worth knowing."

## Acceptance Criteria

- A brief inline comment on the `tr -d` lines in `daemon.sh` notes that tabs are
  intentionally omitted (handled by Python-side strip in parse_session_index).
- `make check` passes.
