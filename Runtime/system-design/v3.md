THE SHIFT
=========

BEFORE:
  pick-role.py SCORES ──> daemon LOADS one operator ──> agent does ALL work
  500 turns, rotten context, wrong role, boundary violations

AFTER:
  dashboard.py BRIEFS ──> brain THINKS ──> sub-agents EXECUTE in worktrees
  brain REVIEWS PRs ──> brain MERGES ──> brain REPORTS
  50 turns Opus + 200 turns Sonnet, fresh context, right decisions
